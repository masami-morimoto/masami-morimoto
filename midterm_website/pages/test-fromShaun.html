WHAT IS KOTO?

Here are the basics knowledge about Koto

Koto has 13 strings and removable bridges can create new note

Koto sheet music

Koto music score is showing the numbers between 1 to 13 in Chinese characters. You are simply playing that particular string.

Tuning

When you play koto, you use TSUME, picks and/ or finger. When you play with picks, it sounds sharp and when you play with finger, it sounds soft like a harp.

Press the strings to create half and full note higher
